About Us

SAISIKA MEC TECHNOLOGY is a multidisciplinary engineering firm specializing in Electrical Engineering, Electronics, Embedded Systems, Software Development, Renewable Energy Solutions, and Technical Training.

We design and build innovative engineering solutions for individuals, businesses, educational institutions, and industries. Our team combines technical expertise with practical experience to deliver reliable, efficient, and affordable solutions. From custom embedded products and software applications to solar energy systems and hands-on engineering training, we help clients transform ideas into reality.
